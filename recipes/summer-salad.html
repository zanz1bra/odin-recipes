Summer salad

Description

This is a simple, yet fancy looking dish. Could be used to possibly make an impression on your potential partner. In that case, though, I recommend not using the onion

A little disclamer - the picture does not represent this dish fully. I stole this picture from the internet. I don't have a camera myself, but the real dish is much more colorful

Ingredients

  • Ice lettuce
  • Red onion (optional)
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatos
  • Olive oil
  • Feta cheese
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cut red onion in little pieces
  2. Tear ice lettuce with fingers
  3. Slice cucumber longways in half and cut in slices (you should get semi circles)
  4. Cut cherry tomatos in half
  5. Cut feta cheese in cubes
  6. Put everything in a bowl
  7. Add 4 teaspoons of olive oil
  8. Add your favorite spices
  9. Mix everything!
  10. Enjoy your summer meal!
